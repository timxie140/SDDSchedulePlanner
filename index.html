What is SchedulePlanner?

Add Courses and Personl Events

You have the ability to rate courses. This helps Rensselaer students learn about the workload of potential future classes. You must be an RPI student in order to rate a class. Simply just login and input the CRN of the course you'd like to rate.

Click here to rate a course

View Classes

You have the ability to look up any course. You can input the course name or course code to find course(s) you desired. And you will then be able to add them to your schedule.

Click here to view a course

Analyze Schedule

You have the ability to view up to 6 courses' ratings with in-depth analysis. Upon entering up to 6 CRNs, you are given the information most crucial for each course. You will also have a average difficulty and cumulative workload based on the course CRN(s) you have inputted.
